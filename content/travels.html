This is my travel bucket list! I had travelled a lot during my gap year, but there are still a lot more places I'd like to explore!


Patagonia

I love hiking, so Patagonia is my dream destination! I'd like to hike the W trek and trails near El Chalten, go see the Glacier Perito Moreno, and visit Ushuaia at the end of the world.

Galapagos Islands

I first learned about the Galapagos Islands because of Darwin's observations here that led him to propose the theory of evolution, and it fascinated me how vastly the ecosystem can differ from island to island. There are many species on Galapagos that are not found anywhere else on Earth . Visit their official website for a more detailed explanation!
