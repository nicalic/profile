Here are some songs from musicals that I love that makes me feel like I'm dreaming!


A Soft Place to Land - Waitress

Waitress has so many beautiful songs, but this song in particular makes me feel like I'm floating in a dream and don't want to wake up. It is an underrated gem out of the Waitress songs.


In A Crowd of Thousands - Anastasia

Coming at nearly the end of the musical, this duet is almost magical in the way it makes its revelation. Incidentally, Anastasia is coming to Sydney next January, so if you're interested, you should come see!


A Dream is A Wish Your Heart Makes - Cinderella

I can't resist adding this footage of Ilene Woods, the original voice actress for Cinderella singing her iconic song here. Her voice has a lovely warmth at the lower tones, which is very rare for a soprano and something I admittedly am envious of.
